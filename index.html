GIRLS CLUB

Girls Club is a comic written by Patt Furie that follows The four girlfriend of boysclub , PEPA, Landa , Bretta, and Andrea, as they navigate the crazy new world of their post-college year lives

Set in a world where the boys are dominating crypto enjoying lavish benders and soaking up all the amenities fame has to offer, they are wives left at home with an ever growing need for something more.

Through a mix of “we’ve had enough” and “watch us do it better” the book Girls Club explores a reality where the girls decide to take on defi themselves with one goal in mind, to dominate it better than the boys ever could.
